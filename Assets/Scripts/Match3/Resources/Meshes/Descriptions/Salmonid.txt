Though never as common as Tui Chub, the bones of salmonids (trout or salmon) have been found in the Pleistocene and early Holocene deposits of the Paisley Caves. They disappeared for a while when Lake Chewaucan was at its most dry, then reappear in small numbers in the late Holocene. Trout bones are difficult to identify to species but at Fossil Lake scientists have found enough bones to know that the Rainbow Trout was present there in the Pleistocene.
